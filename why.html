Why cosmik?

cos ~ Chief of Staff

mik ~ Soumik (me)

cosmik ~ the practice

Reads as cosmic ~ that part is intentional.
A Chief of Staff's job is seeing the whole system from above.

Pipelines that fill themselves

Recruiting, revenue, and retention running as systems ~ not weekly heroics that collapse when one person is sick.

AI that actually ships

Workflows in production inside 90 days, built on tools you already pay for. Not a slide deck about transformation.

One accountable owner

A single person responsible for how the company runs internally ~ so you can stop being that person yourself.

A note on the obvious question

There are plenty of AI chief-of-staff tools now ~ meeting bots, inbox triage, daily briefings. I build with systems like these; I'm not one of them. Tools transcribe your decisions. A Chief of Staff makes them with you, owns the follow-through, and is accountable when it matters. The AI is my leverage. The judgment is the job.
